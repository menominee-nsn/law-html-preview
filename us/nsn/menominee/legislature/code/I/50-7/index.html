Code of the Menominee Indian Tribe

§ 50-7 Documents affected.

The Menominee Tribal Legislature approves and authorizes the Tribal Chairperson to execute all documents necessary to further the rehabilitation of housing contemplated by this project. This approval and authorization includes, but is not limited to:

A. The lease or leases and any amendments to such lease or leases entered into by and between the Menominee Indian Tribe of Wisconsin and Menominee Tribal Housing Limited Partnership No. 7, dated April 8, 2011, pertaining to those properties listed in § 50-10 of this article; and [Amended 7-21-2011]

B. The lease or leases and any amendments to such lease or leases entered into by and between the Menominee Indian Tribe of Wisconsin and Menominee Tribal Housing Limited Partnership No. 8 pertaining to those properties listed in § 50-11 of this article; and [Amended 7-21-2011]

C. A Guaranty Agreement by and between the Menominee Indian Tribe of Wisconsin and an affiliate of Raymond James Tax Credit Fund, Inc., related to Menominee Tribal Housing Limited Partnership No. 7; and

D. A Guaranty Agreement by and between the Menominee Indian Tribe of Wisconsin and an affiliate of Raymond James Tax Credit Fund, Inc., related to Menominee Tribal Housing Limited Partnership No. 8; and

E. A Title Guaranty Agreement by and among the Menominee Indian Tribe of Wisconsin and Menominee Tribal Housing Limited Partnership No. 7; and

F. A Title Guaranty Agreement by and among the Menominee Indian Tribe of Wisconsin and Menominee Tribal Housing Limited Partnership No. 8; and

G. Loans and any amendment to loans entered into by and among the Menominee Indian Tribe of Wisconsin and Menominee Tribal Housing Limited Partnership No. 7.

H. Loans and any amendment to loans entered into by and among the Menominee Indian Tribe of Wisconsin and Menominee Tribal Housing Limited Partnership No. 8.

I. Outside Reserve Pledge Agreement or agreements by and among the Menominee Indian Tribe of Wisconsin and Menominee Tribal Housing Limited Partnership No. 7.

J. Outside Reserve Pledge Agreement or agreements by and among the Menominee Indian Tribe of Wisconsin and Menominee Tribal Housing Limited Partnership No. 8.

K. Any other written agreements authorized by the Menominee Tribal Legislature, and entered into between the Menominee Indian Tribe of Wisconsin and any third party, in furtherance of the acquisition of low-income housing tax credits and necessary actions connected with such acquisition by Menominee Tribal Housing Limited Partnership No. 7 and Menominee Tribal Housing Limited Partnership No. 8. All such agreements, in order to be considered subject to the terms of this article, shall contain a statement that the third party contracting with the Tribe understands and agrees to be subject to the jurisdiction of the Menominee Tribal Courts for the purposes of enforcement of such agreement and this article.
