Code of the Menominee Indian Tribe

§ 98-5 Authority; term of legislative committees.

A. This plan for the administration of the tribal government is adopted pursuant to Article IV, Section 7 of the Tribal Constitution and Bylaws. This plan for the establishment of community and legislative committees of the Tribal Legislature is adopted pursuant to Article IV, Section 6 and Bylaw I, Section 2 of the Constitution and Bylaws, respectively.

B. Legislative committees shall be established for a period of one year; provided, however, that committees shall continue to serve until the Tribal Legislature restructures such committees each year.
