Code of the Menominee Indian Tribe

§ 335-2 Adoption of federal food code.

A. Unless otherwise provided herein, the Tribe hereby adopts the entire 2022 Federal FDA Food Code through incorporation by reference into this law.

B. Should a provision of Chapter 335 conflict with a provision of the Federal FDA Food Code, the provision of Chapter 335 will govern.

C. Copies of the 2022 FDA Food Code adopted pursuant to this chapter are available for inspection at the Office of the Tribal Chairperson.

D. Permit and licensing fees shall be established by the Regulatory Authority and approved by a motion of the Menominee Tribal Legislature. Permit and licensing fees shall be submitted to the Member Services Department at the time of application, renewal, or change of ownership. Permits and licenses are approved by the Regulatory Authority and issued by the Member Services Department.

E. The term "Regulatory Authority," as used in the adopted Food Code, shall mean the Menominee Indian Tribe of Wisconsin Department of Agriculture and Food Systems.

F. The following provisions of the FDA 2022 Food Code are modified as follows:

(1) In Section 8-401.10(A) and (B), the term "six months" is replaced with "12 months."
