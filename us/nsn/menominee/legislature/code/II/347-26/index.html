Code of the Menominee Indian Tribe

§ 347-26 Requirements for sellers of pull-tabs.

To be eligible as a seller of pull-tabs, the following requirements must be met:

A. The seller shall:

(1) Be an enrolled tribal member;

(2) Be certified by the State of Wisconsin as an approved gaming vendor;

(3) Be responsible for the installation of surveillance equipment, including cameras, as required by the Tribal Gaming Commission;

(4) Maintain records of all purchases of pull-tab tickets, sales of pull-tabs, profits from sales of pull-tabs, and other information required by the Tribal/State Compact;

(5) Make payment of 60% of all profits from sales of pull-tabs to the Menominee Tribe;

(6) Provide for audits by a certified public accountant of all financial activities concerning the sale of pull-tabs as requested by the Tribe and in no event on less than an annual basis; and

(7) Make provisions for inspection and monitoring of pull-tab sales at any time by the Tribal Gaming Commission.

B. There shall be licensing by the Tribal Gaming Commission of all persons engaged in the sale of pull-tabs and compliance with Section 542.6 of the Minimum Internal Control Standards.

C. Sale to anyone under the age of 21 years is prohibited.

D. All costs for the foregoing requirements shall be borne by the individual who elects to engage in the sale of pull-tabs.
