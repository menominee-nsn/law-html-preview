Code of the Menominee Indian Tribe

§ 512-8 List of water bodies.

Waters of the Reservation make up part of the Great Lakes basin and drain into Lake Michigan via the Wolf River Lower Green Bay Watershed and the Oconto River Upper Green Bay Watershed. The following two tables list the water bodies within the Reservation according to Subsection A, Lakes, and Subsection B, Rivers, Streams, and Creeks.
